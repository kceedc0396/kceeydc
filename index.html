About James Soko (Kceey Dc)

James Soko, known as Kceey Dc, was born on 25th August, 2000 in Chipata, Zambia. He is currently living in Lusaka. From a young age, he had a strong desire to be part of the entertainment industry.

In 2016, while attending Kamwala Secondary School, he finally started dancing and later joined the Magic Blusters. In 2018, he began writing and releasing his own music. In 2021, he was officially announced as the leader of the Magic Blusters Hip-Hop Dance team.

In 2023, he started pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Cyber Security at Kapasa Makasa University. Alongside his academic pursuits, Kceey Dc is still exploring various aspects of the entertainment industry.

He created The ZED Playlist For Upcoming Artists to support and promote aspiring Zambian artists. Additionally, through Dc MM Tv, he helps individuals who lack access to equipment or guidance in content creation and publishing. Kceey Dc believes in his abilities to succeed, which is why he is known as Kceey the Daily Champion.
